Center Rules

First: absence and delay.

  • The instrument of the center is aware of any delay or absence of the teacher.
  • The student must inform the administration 24 hours before his absence, unless he is ill then he must inform the administration two hours before the lesson.
  • If the student is absent for any reason, the lesson will not be compensated for it.
  • If the student is absent three times without a real excuse, he is prohibited from entering the exam.
  • If the student is late for thirty minutes, he is prohibited from entering the lesson.
  • If the teacher is late, the student waits for thirty minutes, and if he does not come, he will make up for this lesson.
  • If the teacher is absent, the student is given the choice between entering another professor in his place or replacing time with his original teacher (in the private lesson only)

Third: courses

  • Daily homework is done via the site and at the end of the week the weekly homework is sent to the teacher for correction.
  • homeworks represent 30% of the passing score and 70% of the exams.

Fourth: Homeworks

  • After registration, the student is sent the dates of the beginning and end of the course via e-mail.
  • If the student does not register and pay three days before the end of the current course, his place is considered empty, and the center has the right to give it to others, and he has no right to claim this place
  • he times marked by precedence are recorded

Fourth: Refund.

  • The student can redeem in the first week of the course for a compulsive excuse (death, severe illness, ...) after a discount determined by the administration so that it does not harm the center.
  • After the first week, no refund or postponement can be made in any way.
  • The refund does not include the price of books, registration fees, and money transfer expenses

Fifth: Prayer times.

If the prayer occurred during the lesson, the lesson stops for half an hour until the student can perform the prayer, then the lesson resumes after that, and this is not deducted from the student's time.

Sixth: Other.

If the student has a communication problem, he is allowed 30 minutes to solve that problem. If the problem is not solved, then the lesson is canceled and he is not compensated.

If the course ends and the student has abam that he did not study, then he will go to the new course or give him money back these days if he does not want to complete the study at the center.

These lessons are not mixed, and men are strictly prohibited from attending women’s lessons, or vice versa.

Students are prohibited from talking about politics during the lesson.

Students are prohibited from going into legal disagreement during the lesson.

If something goes wrong with the teacher about something, students cannot talk to the teacher about that, and he has to quickly contact the administration and report that error .
